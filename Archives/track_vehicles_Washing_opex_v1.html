Calculation model — how it works
Fuel (per vehicle per year)
Working fuel = (L_line ÷ working_speed) × passes/yr × L/h × qty Transit fuel = (transit_km ÷ 100) × L/100km × passes/yr × qty Total fuel (L) = working_fuel + transit_fuel Fuel cost (€) = total_fuel × fuel_price_per_L
Water — cleaning vehicles only
Water (L/yr) = L_line × passes/yr × L_water/km × qty Water (m³/yr) = water_L ÷ 1000 Water cost (€) = water_m3 × price_per_m3 Note: transit fuel uses L/100km (road mode). Working fuel uses L/h × (L_line ÷ speed). Speed is the operational working speed, not transit.
